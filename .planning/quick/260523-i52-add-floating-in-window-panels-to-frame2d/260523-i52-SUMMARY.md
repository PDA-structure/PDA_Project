# Quick Task 260523-i52: Floating in-window panels for frame2d — Summary

**Status:** complete (UAT-approved 2026-05-23 — same-day execution after planning landed earlier that evening)

Added a float/dock affordance to every card in the frame2d toolbar. Each `<details class="card">` now carries a small Unicode `↗` button in its `<summary>` header. Clicking pops the card to `position: absolute` inside a new `#cardFloatLayer` at top-right of the canvas area; the card is then draggable by its header. Clicking the now-`↙` button returns it to its original toolbar slot. Listener survival is by construction safe (the `<details>` element is moved, not cloned, and existing listeners attach via `getElementById` with no parent traversal).
