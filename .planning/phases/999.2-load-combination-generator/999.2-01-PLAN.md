Linearity basis (solver_core/.../solvers/truss2d.py:114): member force `N = k*(-c*u0 - s*u1 + c*u2 + s*u3)`
is linear in displacement, and `u = Ks^-1 f` is linear in forceVector. Therefore
`N(Σ f_k) = Σ N(f_k)` — superposition over cached per-case member_forces is exact.
