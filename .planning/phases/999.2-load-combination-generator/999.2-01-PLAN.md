Linearity basis (solver_core/.../solvers/truss2d.py:114): member force `N = k*(-c*u0 - s*u1 + c*u2 + s*u3)`
is linear in displacement, and `u = Ks^-1 f` is linear in forceVector. Therefore BOTH
`N(Σ f_k) = Σ N(f_k)` AND `u(Σ f_k) = Σ u(f_k)` — superposition over cached per-case
member_forces AND per-case UG is exact. The δ_max deflection check (D-13) is just the worst
component of the superposed UG for a combination.
